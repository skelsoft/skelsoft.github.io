Pocket Monsters: Johto Releases Today!

SKELSOFT is proud to present the release of our first project: Pocket Monsters: Johto! We hope you enjoy revisiting the world of Pokémon, as if it never left the year 2000.

This project was started long before the foundation of SKELSOFT! Back in 2018, our lead developer had an unscratchable itch to play Pokémon Crystal, a staple game of his that had ran the gauntlet of "multiple plays a year". But this year was different. He noticed the flaws in Game Freak's brilliant sequel, and instead of doing what he did every year and continued playing, he wondered if it was possible to shape the game in a way that paid homage to the original Generation II series. And thus, the JOHTO project was born.

To be at the end of the road is a proud moment for SKELSOFT.

Features

  • Experience the original Johto journey, with its robust post-game adventure given an extra helping!
  • Locations from Red and Blue that were removed have been restored, and some new locations added to open up the overworld. Rediscover the Seafoam Islands, wade through Viridian Forest, or surf over Cherrygrove Bay!
  • New engine mechanics, such as re-use of Repel, unlimited TMs, Running Shoes, and using Rock Smash in overworld sometimes containing items.
  • All 251 Pokémon available for capture, battle, and trading with friends!
  • Original game compatibility! Trade and battle with friends on the original Pokémon Gold, Silver, or Crystal versions!
  • Pokémon Stadium 2 compatible! Finished your two-region adventure? Caught them all? Take your Pokémon champions over to the N64 and become the penultimate Gen II master!
