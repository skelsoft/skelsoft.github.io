2009scape

Return to an authentic remake of 2009-era RuneScape. Re-live fond memories, and remember the legacy of the Gower Brothers.

About the game

2009scape is an open source emulation, capturing the essence of RuneScape HD (2008-2012), focusing on the 2008-2009 slice of the game. SKELSOFT is an active contributor to this gargantuan emulation project.

The live games hosted by Jagex have far diverged from the primordial essence of RuneScape, focusing more on player retention, powercreep, sped-up gameplay loops, and QoL to the point of detriment. 2009scape wishes to honor, and respect, the original visions of the Gower brothers, and return what many people think of as "RuneScape", back to the playing community, and to preserve it for future generations.

Features

  • Experience RuneScape HD, with a fully unified low-fantasy artstyle, and the rendering improvements of the RuneTek 4 engine!
  • Enjoy the final years of the Gower's direction for the game, without the impending doom of EoC!
  • Train all 24 skills, especially Summoning!
  • Single-player one-click-to-run builds for people to get in and get to Scaping as easily as possible!
  • Play through some iconic quests, slay monsters and bosses, and wander through an immersive fantasy world!
  • Nifty client improvements such as increased render distance, and Slayer counters are avaliable for use via toggles!

Open Source

Based on the bones of a former RSPS project, Arios530, 2009scape has come far under proper direction and leadership, with a high focus on respecting the authentic gameplay and mechanics of RSHD-era RuneScape. Hosted on GitLab, you can browse the source, and help contribute to it to make 2009scape the best it can be!
